Privacy Policy

We, the "Rozum" team, respect your privacy and comply with the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and national laws.

1. General Information

This site is a demo example of integrating a voice assistant using OpenAI. We do not collect personal data without your explicit consent.

2. What Data We Process

  • Audio: your voice message is processed via OpenAI Whisper for transcription.
  • Client ID: a unique identifier is stored locally in your browser and used to display your conversation history.
  • Text queries: the text is sent to OpenAI to enable GPT assistant functionality.

This data is not used for profiling or marketing.

3. Technologies and Third-party Services

We use the following technologies to implement functionality:

  • OpenAI API: for transcription, generating replies, and voice responses (TTS).
  • LocalStorage: for storing the client_id in your browser.

Data may be transferred to the USA via OpenAI. We rely on Standard Contractual Clauses (SCC) according to Art. 46 GDPR.

4. Cookies

We do not use advertising or analytics cookies. If a cookie banner is added in the future, you will be able to configure your preferences.
